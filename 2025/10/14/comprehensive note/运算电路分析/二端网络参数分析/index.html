Z参数

$$ \left{
\begin{aligned}
\dot{U}{1}=Z{11}\dot{I}{1}+Z{12}\dot{I}{2}
\ \dot{U}{2}=Z_{21}\dot{I}{1}+Z{22}\dot{I}{2}
\end{aligned}
\right. U=\begin{bmatrix}
\dot{U}{1}\dot{U}{2}
\end{bmatrix}\qquad I=
\begin{bmatrix}
\dot{I}{1}\dot{I}{2}
\end{bmatrix} \qquad Z=\begin{bmatrix}
Z{11};Z_{12}\Z_{21};Z_{22}
\end{bmatrix} U=ZIZ_{12}=Z_{21}$$

Y参数

$$\left {
\begin{aligned}
\dot{I}{1}=Y{11}\dot{U}{1}+Y{12}\dot{U}{2}
\ \dot{I}{2}=Y_{21}\dot{U}{1}+Y{22}\dot{U}{2}
\end{aligned}
\right. Y=\begin{bmatrix}
Y{11};Y_{12}\Y_{21};Y_{22}
\end{bmatrix} I=YU $$
==当Y与Z均存在时==

T参数

$$ \left{ \begin{aligned}
\dot{U}{1}=T{11}\dot{U}{2}-T{12}\dot{I}_2\ \dot{I}1=T{21}\dot{U}2-T{22}\dot{I}_2
\end{aligned}\right.
$$

  • 对于不含受控源的不对称二端口网络(一般可简化成[[图1.png]])

H参数

$$\left{ \begin{aligned}
\dot{U}{1}=H{11}\dot{I}{1}+H{12}\dot{U}_2\ \dot{I}2=H{21}\dot{I}1+H{22}\dot{U}_2
\end{aligned}\right.
$$

  • 对于不含受控源的不对称二端口网络
